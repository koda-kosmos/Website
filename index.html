ELECTRONIC LIVE MUSIC 

Pulsating basslines, nimble beats, hypnotic pads, and organic melodies – a musical cosmos created from a tangle of cables and synthesizers.

Koda Kosmos performs their tracks live, taking the audience on an expedition. With analog sounds, digital textures, and a pinch of chaos, Koda Kosmos creates a sound universe that touches on various spheres of electronic music.

In addition, Koda Kosmos brings along an audio-reactive light installation that expands the spectrum of the music. The aim is to harmonize the space, the people, and the music.
